Chapter one · where I’m from

A small-town kid who got curious and kept going.

I grew up in Bangalore, a vibrant city in Karnataka, in a close, warm family. Curiosity was always the loudest thing in the room — I wanted to know how things worked, why people do what they do, what’s on the other side of the next hill.

That curiosity turned into a Master’s in Data Science and a career building AI in Atlanta. New country, new city, a lot of learning the hard way — but I carried home with me: the values, the people, the idea that you stay grounded no matter how far you travel.
